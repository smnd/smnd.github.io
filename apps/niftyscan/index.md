# NiftyScan

> See exactly what's inside any payment QR code.

NiftyScan parses SGQR, PayNow, DuitNow, and UPI codes to show merchant name, UEN, amount, and other embedded fields in plain English — so you can verify before you pay. Scan Wi-Fi QR codes and join the network in one tap. Works offline. No accounts. No tracking.

Also scans any standard QR code, URL, or barcode, with a clean on-device history.

[![Download on the App Store](https://developer.apple.com/assets/elements/badges/download-on-the-app-store.svg)]({{ site.data.links.niftyscan.download_url }})

**There are a million QR scanners out there. How is this different?**

- **Private by design**: All scans and history stay on device. No tracking. No ads.
- **Payment QR parsing**: Human-readable breakdown for SGQR/PayNow, DuitNow, UPI — see the merchant name, proxy type, UEN, amount, and reference fields.
- **Wi-Fi QR support**: Scan, see the network details, and join with one tap. Pin Wi-Fi codes for quick access later.
- **Clean history**: Search, copy, share, clear anytime.
- **Pro tools**: Unlimited history, App Lock, Pinned scans.

**How does the app work?**

1. Open and point the camera; auto-detects QR & barcodes.
2. See raw data and, when applicable, a readable payment or Wi-Fi breakdown.
3. Tap to copy, share, open links, or join Wi-Fi.
4. Manage history; with Pro you can pin and lock.

**Who is this app for?**

- Travellers and expats using Wise or international banking apps who need to extract UEN or merchant details from Singapore QR codes.
- Shoppers who want a fast, private scanner without data leaving their phone.
- Small businesses, fintech builders, and curious minds who want to understand what's inside the QR.

**What does PRO Include?**

Pay once, use forever. Not a subscription. All future pro features included.

- Unlimited history (free version limited to 10 most recent scans)
- App Lock with Face ID
- Pinned scans that survive bulk clears
